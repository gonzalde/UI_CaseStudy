Bank Hotel

Östermalm Stockholm

Per Night

253€

This elegant hotel is set in an ideal central Stockholm location, steps from Östermalm district's notable Strandvägen and Stureplan. The Bank Hotel features an on-site Mediterranean restaurant and fitness centre, along with free WiFi. Kungsträdgården Metro Station is right around the corner, 100 m away. Housed in a former bank building, all rooms are designed with stylish furnishings and feature a flat-screen TV, minibar and coffee facilities. Bathrobes, slippers and plush arm chairs provide further relaxation. Other details include luxury toiletries in the marble bathrooms.
